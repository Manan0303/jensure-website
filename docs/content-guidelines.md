# Content Guidelines

## Positioning

Jensure is AI Operational Infrastructure. Not a chatbot company. Not a consulting firm. Not a generic automation agency.

Every sentence on the site must reflect this positioning.

## Tone

- Direct and operational
- Confident without being boastful
- Specific over vague
- Systems-minded

## What to write

| Instead of... | Write... |
|---------------|----------|
| "Revolutionary AI solutions" | "AI systems that remove manual work from your operations" |
| "Cutting-edge technology" | "Agent systems that coordinate and execute tasks automatically" |
| "We help businesses grow" | "We eliminate the operational tasks that slow your team down" |
| "Powerful automation" | "Automation that runs reporting, follow-ups, and data entry without human input" |
| "AI-powered platform" | "AI Departments that operate your business functions" |

## Headline Formula

Lead with the operational outcome, not the technology.

Good:
- "Your operations, automated."
- "Run your business with AI Departments."
- "Eliminate repetitive work. Run on systems."

Avoid:
- "The future of AI is here"
- "Unlock the power of automation"
- "Transform your business with AI"

## Copy Structure

Every section should answer one of:
1. What problem does this solve?
2. How does it work specifically?
3. What is the measurable outcome?

## Numbers and specifics

When claiming outcomes, be specific:
- "Weekly reports generated in 2 minutes" not "faster reporting"
- "Saves 20 hours per week per employee" not "significant time savings"
- "Automates 80% of lead follow-up" not "improved follow-up"

## What Jensure never says

- Revolutionary
- Game-changing
- Cutting-edge
- Transformative
- Best-in-class
- Next-generation
- Empower
- Leverage
- Seamless (unless technically accurate)
