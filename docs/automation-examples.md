# Automation Examples

## Healthcare

- Patient intake forms automatically parsed and entered into EHR system — eliminates 45 minutes of admin work per patient
- Appointment reminders sent via SMS and email on a schedule — reduces no-show rate by 30%
- Insurance pre-authorization requests generated and submitted automatically from appointment data
- Clinical notes summarized from transcription — physician review time cut from 20 minutes to 3 minutes per patient

## Manufacturing

- Daily production reports generated from floor sensor data — no manual entry required
- Inventory levels synced across warehouse and ERP systems every 15 minutes
- Supplier purchase orders generated automatically when stock thresholds are hit
- Weekly reports emailed to management generated in under 2 minutes with no human input

## Financial Services

- New client onboarding documents collected, verified, and filed automatically
- KYC checks run against databases on submission — compliance team only reviews flagged cases
- Monthly compliance reports generated from transaction logs without analyst input
- Client portfolio performance reports emailed automatically on a weekly schedule

## Marketing Agencies

- Weekly client performance reports pulled from ad accounts, GA4, and CRM — compiled and emailed automatically
- Blog content brief generated from keyword research data within minutes of a new topic request
- Social media calendar populated from approved content briefs — posts scheduled across all platforms
- Lead response emails sent within 5 minutes of inbound form submission

## Ecommerce

- Product listings synced across Shopify, Amazon, and Etsy from a single source of truth
- Order confirmation and shipping update emails triggered automatically from fulfillment events
- Return requests processed and replacement orders triggered without customer service involvement
- Weekly revenue reports with channel breakdown emailed to founders every Monday at 8am

## Recruitment Firms

- Resumes screened against job criteria — only qualified candidates forwarded to consultants
- Interview invitations sent and calendar slots booked automatically upon candidate shortlisting
- Follow-up messages sent to candidates who haven't responded within 48 hours
- Weekly pipeline report showing active roles, candidates in progress, and offers made generated automatically

## SaaS Companies

- New user onboarding sequence triggered automatically on signup — product tips, setup guides, check-in emails
- Churn risk signals (low login frequency, failed payments) flagged and escalated to success team automatically
- Support tickets tagged, categorized, and routed to the correct team without manual triage
- Monthly MRR and churn reports generated from Stripe data without analyst involvement
