# Jensure Services

## 1. AI Departments

Full department builds. Each includes a Master Agent and multiple specialized Sub Agents. See architecture.md for full department breakdowns.

Available departments:
- AI Marketing Department
- AI Sales Department
- AI Operations Department
- AI Finance Department
- AI HR & Recruitment Department

## 2. GTM Systems

Go-to-market automation systems that operate within the AI Marketing Department. See gtm-systems.md for detail.

Key components:
- Lead generation automation
- Email outreach systems
- LinkedIn outreach automation
- Lead enrichment pipelines
- CRM integration and automation
- Meeting scheduling systems
- Automated follow-up sequences
- Campaign analytics dashboards

## 3. Small Automation Solutions

Targeted automations that solve specific operational tasks without requiring a full AI Department.

| Automation | What it does |
|------------|-------------|
| Data Entry Automation | Eliminates manual form and spreadsheet entry |
| Spreadsheet Synchronization | Keeps data consistent across multiple sheets/systems |
| Meeting Transcription & Summarization | Records, transcribes, and summarizes meetings automatically |
| Email Inbox Management | Categorizes, routes, and drafts email responses |
| Invoice Generation | Automatically creates and sends invoices from triggers |
| Document Summarization | Extracts key information from contracts, reports, documents |
| Customer Feedback Analysis | Aggregates and categorizes feedback from multiple channels |
| Content Repurposing Automation | Converts long-form content into multiple formats |
| Task Creation from Communications | Converts Slack/email messages into tasks automatically |
