# GTM Systems

## What Are GTM Systems?

GTM (Go-To-Market) Systems are automated pipelines that generate and manage leads across multiple channels — without requiring manual outreach or follow-up by sales staff.

They operate as a subsystem within the AI Marketing Department, coordinated by the CMO Agent.

## Components

| Component | What it does |
|-----------|-------------|
| Lead Generation Automation | Identifies and captures leads from inbound channels, web activity, and intent signals |
| Email Outreach System | Sends personalized outreach sequences with automated follow-up logic |
| LinkedIn Outreach Automation | Manages connection requests, messages, and follow-ups on LinkedIn at scale |
| Lead Enrichment Pipeline | Automatically researches leads and appends company, role, and contact data |
| CRM Integration | Syncs all lead and deal data into the client's CRM without manual entry |
| Meeting Scheduling System | Automates calendar booking from outreach sequences |
| Follow-Up Sequences | Ensures no lead goes cold with timed, personalized follow-ups |
| Campaign Analytics Dashboard | Tracks open rates, reply rates, meetings booked, and pipeline value |

## How It Fits the AI Marketing Department

## Use Cases

**SaaS Company:** Automates outbound prospecting — identifies ICP accounts, enriches contact data, sends personalized sequences, books demo calls. Sales team only handles qualified meetings.

**Marketing Agency:** Runs new business development on autopilot — LinkedIn outreach to CMOs, email sequences with case study assets, automated follow-ups, meeting booked directly to founder's calendar.

**Ecommerce Brand:** Manages affiliate and partnership outreach — identifies potential partners, sends outreach, tracks responses, books calls.
